Fettuccine Alfredo

The Dish

This dish is delicious and YUUUUMY!!! The dish pairs well with chicken or broccolis if you are feeling really hungry. Make sure to get the right cheese though or it might taste like a cheese pizza!

Ingredients

  • 24 oz dry fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup butter
  • 3/4 pint heavy cream
  • 1 pinch garlic salt
  • 3/4 cup grated Romano cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesian cheese

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil. Cook fettuccine at a boil until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes. Drain.
  3. Heat butter and cream in a large saucepan over low heat until butter melted; add garlic salt, salt, and black pepper.
  4. Increase the heat to medium; stir in grated Romano and Parmesan cheeses until melted and sauce has thickened.
  5. Add cooked pasta to sauce; toss until thoroughly coated. Serve immediately.
